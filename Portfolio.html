About Me

I’m Riya Verma, a passionate Graphic Designer and 3D Modeler with a keen eye for detail and a drive to create visually stunning and functional designs. My journey in design has equipped me with a diverse skill set, enabling me to deliver high-quality work across various mediums.

Skills:

  • Graphic Design: Proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDRAW, and other graphic design tools to create compelling visuals and branding materials.
  • 2D Design: Expertise in designing eye-catching graphics, including advertisements, posters, and promotional materials.
  • 3D Modeling: Skilled in using Autodesk 3ds Max, SketchUp, and Lumion to craft detailed and realistic 3D models, from conceptual interiors to intricate product designs.
  • Animation: Knowledgeable in Adobe After Effects for creating dynamic animations and visual effects to enhance digital content.
  • Creative Problem-Solving: Adept at translating complex ideas into clear and engaging visual solutions, tailored to meet specific project goals.

With a blend of creativity and technical proficiency, I strive to push the boundaries of design and deliver exceptional results that resonate with audiences and stand out in a competitive landscape. My work is driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, ensuring that each project not only meets but exceeds expectations.
